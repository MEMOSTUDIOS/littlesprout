The Game

Welcome to the magical land of Romania, where nature blooms in its most spectacular forms!

In the heart of enchanted forests, sparkling rivers and vibrant meadows, a small sprout named Little Sprout is about to embark on a great adventure.
Guided by the whispers of nature, Little Sprout seeks to learn about the most famous animals, insects, flowers, fish, birds and butterflies of Romania.

Each turn brings a new discovery as you draw cards featuring Romania's most incredible natural wonders.

How to Play

Game setup: 2-10 players

1. Choose your own pawn.

2. Each player starts with 6 cards from the pack.

3. Roll the dice and place the pawn on the number shown.

4. Choose an option and play a card: draw, memorize, interpret.

5. Advance 1 or 2 points if someone guessed correctly.

6. The player who guessed correctly also advances 1 point.

7. The played card is placed in the deck and shuffled.

8. A new card is drawn at the end of the turn.

9. Players play the color option they advanced onto.

Gameplay

Players take turns playing cards to build their knowledge tree.

Choose an option to play:

1. Drawing (+1 point)

2. Memorizing 2 facts (+2 points)

3. Interpretation (+1 point)

The first player to guess correctly wins 1 point.

Winning

The first player to reach "little sprout" level wins! But wait, there is more!

After reaching the "little sprout" level, players can continue playing from the beginning or form teams with other players. Teams can be formed with a maximum of 2-3 players.
